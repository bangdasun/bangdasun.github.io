About Mathematics Formula Display

2017-08-22

A note about the display of mathematical formula in my posts…

I use Mathjax to write formulas in posts, however this could happens:

it just show me the TeX code …:-(

That’s one way to resolve this, go to the top right of the browser, click the shield and then click “Load unsafe scripts”. Don’t worry, it’s really safe :-)

Then we will get what we want exactly.
